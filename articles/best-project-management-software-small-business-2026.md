## How to Choose the Right PM Software for Your Small Business

### Step 1: Define Your Team Size
- **1-5 people**: Trello, Notion, or Basecamp (all affordable or free)
- **5-20 people**: ClickUp, Asana, or Monday.com (best value for growing teams)
- **20-50 people**: Monday.com, Wrike, or Jira (scalable with advanced features)

### Step 2: Identify Your Work Style
- **Visual/Kanban lovers**: Trello, Monday.com
- **Structured/timeline-driven**: Asana, Wrike
- **Agile/Scrum teams**: Jira, ClickUp
- **Documentation-heavy**: Notion, Basecamp

### Step 3: Consider Your Budget
- **$0/month**: ClickUp (free plan), Asana (free, 10 users), Trello (free, 10 boards)
- **Under $50/month**: Basecamp ($15 flat), Trello Business ($5/seat), Notion ($10/seat)
- **Under $200/month**: Monday.com Pro, Asana Premium, ClickUp Business

### Step 4: Check Integrations
Ensure the tool integrates with your existing stack:
- **Google Workspace**: All tools support it
- **Slack**: All support native Slack integration
- **Developer tools**: Jira and ClickUp have the best GitHub/GitLab integration
- **CRM**: Asana and Wrike integrate well with Salesforce and HubSpot

## FAQ: Project Management Software for Small Business

### Q: Which project management tool is best for a 5-person startup?
**A**: ClickUp or Asana offer excellent free plans for teams of 5. ClickUp's free plan includes unlimited tasks and users, while Asana's free plan supports up to 10 users with core features.

### Q: Is Monday.com worth the price for a small business?
**A**: Yes — if you value visual project management and automation. The $10/seat/month price is competitive, and the automation recipes alone can save hours per week.

### Q: Can I use Notion as my only project management tool?
**A**: Absolutely — many small businesses run entirely on Notion. It works well for task management, documentation, and collaboration. The trade-off is less structured project tracking compared to dedicated tools like Asana or Monday.com.

### Q: What's the cheapest project management software for 15 people?
**A**: Basecamp at $15/month (flat rate) is the cheapest for 15 people. ClickUp Business at $7/seat/month ($105 total) is also competitive.

### Q: Do these tools integrate with accounting software?
**A**: Teamwork integrates natively with QuickBooks and Xero. Most others connect via Zapier.

### Q: How do I migrate from Trello to Monday.com?
**A**: Both tools offer native import tools. Monday.com has a dedicated Trello importer that migrates boards, cards, and labels automatically.

## Pro Tips for Maximizing Project Management ROI

1. **Start with 3 templates** — Don't build from scratch. Use built-in templates and customize as you go.
2. **Set up automations early** — Automate status changes, notifications, and recurring tasks.
3. **Create a project charter** — Define scope, stakeholders, and milestones before adding tasks.
4. **Review weekly** — Spend 15 minutes every Monday reviewing overdue tasks and adjusting timelines.
5. **Train the team** — Most tools offer free webinars. Get everyone trained within the first week.
