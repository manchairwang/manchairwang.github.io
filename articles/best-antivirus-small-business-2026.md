## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I use consumer antivirus for my small business?
**No.** Consumer antivirus lacks centralized management, compliance reporting, and multi-device licensing. It also violates most consumer license agreements when used commercially.

### Q: How many devices do I need to cover?
Every device that accesses company data: employee laptops, company phones, servers, and even point-of-sale systems. Don't forget contractor devices if they access internal systems.

### Q: Do I need both antivirus and a VPN?
Yes. Antivirus protects against malware, while a VPN encrypts your internet connection. For remote workers on public WiFi, both are essential. Norton 360 bundles both in one subscription.

### Q: What's the minimum protection budget?
Budget at least **$50–$100/month** for a 10-person team. This covers antivirus, basic email security, and central management. Skimping on security costs 100x more in a breach.

### Q: How often should I update antivirus software?
Business-grade solutions update **automatically every few hours**. Just ensure auto-update is enabled in your management console and devices are set to receive updates promptly.
