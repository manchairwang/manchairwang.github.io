## How to Choose the Right Website Builder

### Consider Your Needs:
1. **Budget**: If cost is primary concern, GoDaddy ($10.99/mo) or WordPress.com Starter ($9/mo) are most affordable
2. **Design**: For stunning visuals out of the box, choose Squarespace or Webflow
3. **E-commerce**: Shopify is the gold standard; Wix Business is a solid alternative
4. **SEO**: WordPress.com with Yoast offers the most powerful SEO tools
5. **Scalability**: Webflow and WordPress.com grow best with your business

### Quick Recommendations:
- **Under $20/month**: GoDaddy Premium or WordPress.com Starter
- **Best overall value**: Wix Core ($29/month)
- **Best for online stores**: Shopify Basic ($29/month)
- **Best for design portfolios**: Squarespace Personal ($16/month)
- **Best for growth**: Webflow CMS ($23/month)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I switch website builders later?**
A: Yes, but it requires rebuilding. Start with a platform that can scale with you — Webflow or WordPress.com are the most flexible long-term.

**Q: Do I need coding skills?**
A: No. Wix, Squarespace, GoDaddy, and Shopify require zero coding. Webflow and WordPress.com offer advanced options if you want them.

**Q: Which builder is best for SEO?**
A: WordPress.com with Yoast SEO plugin offers the most comprehensive SEO toolkit. Wix's SEO Wiz is excellent for beginners.

**Q: Can I sell products on any of these?**
A: All six support e-commerce, but Shopify and Wix Business have the most robust features for product sales.

**Q: Do these include hosting?**
A: Yes — all six platforms include managed hosting with their plans. No separate hosting purchase needed.
