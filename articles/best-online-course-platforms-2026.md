### Q: Which platform pays instructors the most?
Teachable and Thinkific both let you keep 100% of revenue (on paid plans). Udemy pays up to 97% for organic sales but only 25% for ads-driven sales.

Absolutely! Many successful instructors use Udemy for exposure and Teachable for their premium offerings.

Top Udemy instructors earn $100K+/year. Teachable/Thinkific creators with strong marketing can earn $200K+. Most beginners earn $500-2000/month within 6 months.

Yes — Teachable, Thinkific, and Udemy all offer free plans. You can start teaching today with zero investment.
